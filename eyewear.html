Eyewear that fits your lifestyle

Express yourself through frames and lenses that are as unique as you are.

Frames

Make the right statement with our hand-picked collection. Our optical frame specialists are patient, honest, and fashion forward. We listen to your needs and goals for your eyewear and will not fit you in a frame unless it's perfect for you.

Choose from our curated selection of independent frame lines rest assured that you are wearing the best quality eyewear.

Sunglasses

Don’t overlook this critical component of your eye health. Include a good pair of sunglasses in your daily routine to deflect damaging UV rays, prevent permanent damage to your retina, lens as well as the sensitive skin around your eyes.

Polarized lenses can help eliminate the glare of sun or snow. Our optical features some of the most premium sun wear available. Let us help you choose the right type of sun wear for your prescription and non prescription needs.

Lenses

With so many options to choose from, our doctors and opticians will take the time to walk you through the best, quality lens options for your unique prescription and lifestyle.

Whether you need single vision lenses, bifocals, "no line bifocals" progressive lenses, anti-fatigue lenses and myopia progression therapy lenses such as MiyoSmart, all our lenses include anti-reflective coating and anti-scratch coating to give you the best vision possible.

Add-on options such as photogrey, tinted lenses and blue light blocking coatings are available to mitigate increased device usage throughout your day.

Same Day Lens Service

Whether you need a quick lens fix or an emergency replacement, our same-day lens service can get you looking sharp, faster. Request this service when you come in for your eye assessment.

Online Service Package

Fell in love with a pair of frames online? Ask about our online service package to ensure your purchase fits perfectly. You’ll receive your measurement details and notations so you can order your eyewear with confidence.

Contact Lenses

A proper fitting and assessment ensures the best comfort and health for your eyes. Select from our quality lens options for your daily, bi-weekly, or monthly replacement needs.

Unique needs such as multifocal lenses and myopia progression control can be addressed at your assessment. For all new wearers, we offer one on one sessions with one of our experienced staff to give safe insertion and removal techniques.

For Dry Eyes

Pollution, climate, and aging can irritate and dry out essential moisture in your eyes. To combat this effectively, our optometrists will prescribe a dry eye treatment routine to relieve discomfort and alleviate irritation.

Select from our high-quality, preservative-free artificial tears, lid cleaning products and moist heat eye compresses to round out your daily eye care routine.

Our specialty brands include Hylo, Hylo Dual, Ocunox, Hyabak, Thealoz Duo, Blephagel, Blephaclean Lid Wipes, Blephadex Lid Wipes and Bruder’s Eye Mask, and more.
